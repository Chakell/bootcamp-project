Bootcamp 2021: Clocks in 4 cities

This Processing project demonstrates a comprehensive understanding of the use of classes and objects. The programme displays the time of four cities, intitially the time and city is out of scope of the display, to view the time of the city, you need to move your mouse to a part of the screen to view the time. The focus of the city is based on your mouse position.
